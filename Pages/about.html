Our Story

Fragrance Should Never
Be a Privilege

Qton Perfumes was born in the heart of Nairobi CBD with a simple belief — everyone deserves access to authentic, beautiful fragrance, whether it's a luxury exclusive or an everyday favourite.

🏺
How It Started

Built From the Streets
of Nairobi CBD

Qton Perfumes started with a single idea: Nairobi deserved a fragrance store that didn't compromise on authenticity, regardless of budget. We stock everything from rare luxury exclusives to beloved everyday scents — for men, women, and everyone in between.

What we don't have in stock, we'll source for you. Every bottle that leaves our store — or arrives at your door — is 100% genuine, sourced directly from trusted distributors.

Whether you're dressing for a boardroom, a first date, or simply treating yourself, we believe there's a fragrance that speaks for you — and we're here to help you find it.
